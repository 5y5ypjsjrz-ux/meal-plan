Meal Prep Tips & Success Strategies

Sunday Batch Cooking Strategy (2-3 hours)

  1. Start proteins first - Longest cooking time. Bake chicken/fish while prepping vegetables.
  2. Multi-task with grains - Cook rice/quinoa while proteins bake. Use rice cooker for hands-off cooking.
  3. Roast vegetables - Use multiple sheet pans in oven at different temps.
  4. Make sauces last - While everything cools, prep dressings and sauces.

Storage Tips

  • Proteins: 4-5 days refrigerated, or freeze half for later
  • Cooked grains: 5-6 days in airtight container
  • Roasted vegetables: 4-5 days
  • Hard-boiled eggs: 7 days in shell
  • Sauces/dressings: 5-7 days in small jars

Time-Saving Hacks

  • Pre-chop vegetables on Sunday - store in containers with damp paper towels
  • Spiralize all zucchini at once, store with paper towel to absorb moisture
  • Make double batches of marinades and sauces - they keep 5-7 days
  • Cook proteins plain, then season differently for variety throughout week
  • Freeze individual portions of soups, curries, and meatballs for future weeks
