🌿 30-Day Menopause Meal Plan for Two

High Protein (80-100g per person) | 1200-1500 Calories per person | 10am-8pm Eating Window

Welcome to Your 30-Day Meal Plan!

Plan Highlights

All recipes serve 2 people. This plan is designed specifically for menopause support with optimal nutrition for hormone balance, energy, and sustainable weight management.

Menopause-Optimized

High in omega-3s, phytoestrogens, calcium, and fiber to support hormone balance and reduce symptoms.

Hawaii-Friendly

Features local fish (ahi, opah, mahi-mahi), Asian greens, and tropical fruits available in Hawaii.

Batch Prep Friendly

Weekend prep guides make weekday meals quick and easy (10-30 minutes).

Bold Flavors

Diverse cuisines: Asian, Indian, Mediterranean, Mexican - nothing boring!

Daily Supplements (Per Person)

Not included in calorie counts - add 40-80 calories per person

  • Collagen peptides: 10-20g (add to morning coffee, smoothies, or soups)
  • Creatine monohydrate: 5g (mix into smoothie or water)

How to Use This Plan

  1. Daily Meals: See all 30 days of complete meal details
  2. Weekly Prep: Sunday batch cooking instructions
  3. Shopping Lists: Accurate quantities for each week
  4. Freeze Ahead: Optional batch cooking for future weeks

Per Person Daily Targets

  • Calories: 1,200-1,500 per day
  • Protein: 80-100g per day
  • Fiber: 25-35g per day
  • Meals: 2 main meals + 2 snacks per day
