Meal Prep Tips & Success Strategies

Sunday Batch Cooking Strategy (2-3 hours)

  1. Start proteins first - Longest cooking time
  2. Multi-task with grains - Cook while proteins bake
  3. Roast vegetables - Use multiple sheet pans
  4. Make sauces last - While everything cools

Storage Tips

  • Proteins: 4-5 days refrigerated, or freeze half
  • Cooked grains: 5-6 days in airtight container
  • Roasted vegetables: 4-5 days
  • Hard-boiled eggs: 7 days in shell
  • Sauces/dressings: 5-7 days in small jars
